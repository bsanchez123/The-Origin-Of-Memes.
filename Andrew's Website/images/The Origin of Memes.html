The Origin of Memes

By: Andrew Johnson

Memes have played a big part in modern culture; but where did they originate, and how did they grow? What happens to a meme when it has been around for too long, and what are the newest and most modern memes today?

This picture you see here is known as the first meme ever! It reads: "How you think you look when a flashlight is taken... How you really look." it was published in a 1921 edition of satirical magazine The Judge. Although this meme isn't well known in modern times, it kick started many of the funny memes we love today.
